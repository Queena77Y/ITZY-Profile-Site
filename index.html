ITZY

Itzy (Stylized as ITZY) is a South Korean girl group formed by JYP Entertainment, consisting of members Yeji, Lia, Ryujin, Chaeryeong, and Yuna. The group debuted on February 11th, 2019, with the release of their single album, It'z Different.

History

2019:

Debut with It'z Different and It'z Icy

2020:

United States tour and It'z Me
